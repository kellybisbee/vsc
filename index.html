Victoria Station Cafe

We love our community at Victoria Station Cafe - from locals in Putnam and everyone visiting us for the first time, we want everyone to feel like they are a part of our VSC family.

Enjoy a homemade pastry, relax with a cup of coffee and we’ll see you ‘round the cafe!

The Loft @ VSC

The Loft @ VSC showcases the talents of our local musicians, poets, and artists. Our weekly Open Mic Night is every Thursday, and our Poetry Reading is the first Wednesday of every month.
